About HollowManRent

Nepal's trusted car rental service since 2015.

OUR STORY

A Decade of Service

Founded in 2015 in Kathmandu, HollowManRent began with just 5 vehicles and a goal to make car rental easy and affordable in Nepal. Today we operate over 100 vehicles across 8 locations in Kathmandu Valley, Pokhara, and Chitwan.

Our commitment to well-maintained vehicles, fair pricing, and excellent customer service has earned us the trust of over 10,000 customers including tourists, businesses, and families across Nepal.

Our Mission & Values

Customer First
