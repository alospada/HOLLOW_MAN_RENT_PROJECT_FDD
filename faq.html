Booking

Making a reservation is simple. Browse our fleet on the Listings page, choose your vehicle, then click Book. Fill in your full name, email, phone number, pick-up location, pick-up date, and return date on the Booking page, then click Confirm Booking. Our team will contact you shortly to finalise pickup details.
Yes. Cancellations made 48 hours or more before your pick-up time are fully refunded. For cancellations within 48 hours, a partial fee may apply. To modify dates, your vehicle, or pick-up location, please contact our support team or visit your profile page, and we'll update your reservation promptly.
You will need:
  • A valid government-issued photo ID (citizenship card or passport)
  • A valid driver's licence (Nepali or international)
  • A valid visa if you are a foreign national
  • Your booking confirmation (email or screenshot)
Please bring originals — photocopies are not accepted at pickup.

Pricing

No hidden fees — ever. The price you see on our Pricing page and vehicle listings is the final daily rate. The only additional costs are fuel (vehicles must be returned with a full tank) and any optional add-ons such as a driver or GPS device, which are stated upfront.
Yes! Weekly bookings receive a 15% discount off the standard daily rate. Monthly corporate or long-term leases are available with further discounts — contact us for a tailored quote. Our Long-Term Leasing service is designed for businesses and extended travellers who need reliable wheels for weeks or months at a time.
We accept cash (NPR), bank transfer, eSewa, Khalti, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically collected at the pickup location. For corporate accounts and long-term leases, monthly invoicing can be arranged.

Insurance

Yes. All rentals include at minimum basic third-party insurance. Our Standard and Premium plans include full comprehensive insurance covering collision, theft, and third-party liability. The Budget plan includes basic insurance — you can upgrade to full coverage for a small additional daily fee.
In the event of an accident or damage, please call our 24/7 roadside assistance line immediately. Do not move the vehicle if it is unsafe to do so. Our team will arrange towing and repair. Renters on full insurance plans have minimal or zero liability for covered incidents. Renters on basic insurance plans may be responsible for an excess charge — details are provided at pickup.
No. Fuel is not included in any plan or insurance policy. Vehicles are delivered with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank. If a vehicle is returned with less than a full tank, a refuelling fee will be charged at the prevailing pump rate plus a small service fee.

We offer 24/7 roadside assistance across Nepal. If you experience a breakdown, flat tyre, or any mechanical issue, call our emergency support number provided in your booking confirmation. We'll dispatch a technician or arrange a replacement vehicle as quickly as possible — at no additional charge for mechanical faults that are not caused by the renter.
